Open Reel Audio - Leader

Leader is used to protect the tape and can also be a place where pertinent information about a tape can be written. It is generally made of uncoated polyester tape. As tape is played back, it is most likely to be damaged at the beginning and end of the tape. Leader should be fairly long (enough to wrap around the reel a few times) so that there is sufficient leader to protect the tape at the head and the tail. If there are any issues with the playback equipment, the leader would ideally take the brunt of the damage. Damaged leader is easier to replace than unique sections of tape. Dirty or damaged leaders should be removed and replaced. If replacing leader which contains information, transcribe the information onto the new leader.
