Labeling

If your item has any kind of labeling on the container, the item itself, or any related material we highly recommend that you return to the General tab under Assessments Manager, and enter this information in the notes field.

Labeling on a container or leader, if it is correct, can offer important clues about what is on the film. If you are replacing a container or leader which holds labeling information, it is important to transfer this information to the new container or label. As advised by the Film Preservation Guide, be sure to copy down any titles, dates or production data found on these items and save notes housed in the can. The guide also warns that container labels should be used with caution as cans frequently are reused or switched by accident.
