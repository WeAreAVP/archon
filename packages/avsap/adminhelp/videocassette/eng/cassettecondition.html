Cartridge and Cassette-Based Video - Cassette Condition

Cassette Inspection At-A-Glance:

  • look for cracks, stress fractures
  • look for dust, dirt, mold or other contaminant which can affect the tape

You're looking for cracks, stress fractures, dust, and so on. Likewise, a dirty, dusty or moldy tape can transmit filth into the playback machine, which would impair the machine's ability to play back the best possible image and sound. Remember, one of your jobs with regard to audiovisual preservation is to try to ensure the best possible image and/or sound quality during playback!
