Disk - Correct Orientation in Storage

Similarly sized discs should be stored together vertically. Shelves should have full-height and full-depth dividers spaced 4 to 6 inches apart, secured at the top and bottom (Library of Congress, 2002). Grooved discs are heavy and can exert pressure—and warp—if too many discs are shelved together without dividers.
