General Condition of Cassette or Cartridge

Cassette Inspection At-A-Glance—Look for:

  • cracks, stress fractures
  • dust, dirt, mold or other contaminant which can affect the tape

A dirty, dusty, or moldy tape transmits filth into the playback machine, impairing it. Look for cracks, stress fractures, dust and other contaminants on the outer shell of the case. Remember, one of your jobs with regard to audiovisual preservation is to try to ensure the best possible image and/or sound quality during playback.
