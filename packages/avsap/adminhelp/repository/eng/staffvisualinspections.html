Visual Inspections

Visual diagnostic inspections of audiovisual media can identify problems such as pest exposure, mold, and physical damage, and can be very effective in assessing the condition of AV items. Visual inspections are also safer than playback inspections; playback inspections always put an AV item at risk for damage, even if the item and playback deck are in good condition. Unfortunately, the intricacies of audiovisual formats often make it so that even if an AV item has been visually inspected and found to be in good order, it could still have poor image and/or sound in playback. Thus, a playback inspection can often be more diagnostic than a simple visual inspection. However, while AvSAP recognizes this, because of the potential for damage, we do not recommend playback inspections.
