Equipment Manuals

One of the best ways to troubleshoot or maintain your equipment in-house is by consulting the manuals that came with the equipment. Most of these manuals are difficult to procure on their own, so it is best to keep the manuals in a safe place whenever you acquire equipment. Should you need to find manuals for your old equipment, one excellent resource is Sam's Technical Publishing, a company that sells, among other things, manuals for various kinds of equipment, including audiovisual playback devices. Their website is http://www.samswebsite.com/. Another possibility for finding equipment manuals is posting a request to the listservs for the Association of Moving Image Archivists ( http://www.amianet.org/) or the Association of Recorded Sound Collections ( http://www.arsc-audio.org/).
